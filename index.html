Privacy Policy – PlankTon

Effective date: November 22, 2025

1. Introduction

PlankTon is a workout timer app (e.g., for planks). We do not collect any personal data. All settings are stored locally on your device and are optionally synchronized with your Apple Watch only.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Custom sounds uploaded by the user are stored locally only and are never sent to any server.

3. Apple Watch Synchronization

When using the companion Apple Watch app, settings (e.g., timer duration and selected sounds) are exchanged via Apple’s WatchConnectivity framework. This communication is end-to-end encrypted and takes place exclusively between your own devices. Apple acts as the data processor in this case (see Apple’s Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

4. Analytics & Advertising

PlankTon contains no analytics tools, trackers, or advertisements.

5. Children

PlankTon does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your country).

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be published on this page.
